About Us

Our Values

Our values at Robotics Daily is to give our readers top of the line stories regarding everything about Tech and Robotics. All of these ideas coming from various sources and companies such as BostonDynamics and many more. Robotics Daily also offers subscriptions to our daily news about Robots. All of these aspects combined is what makes us unique compared to the other forms of news.

Covered Materials

We tend to cover information from sources that sell robots or manufacture at either a consumer level or comercial level. We also tend to show news that either directly relate to Robotics or have a key component that either assists robotic technology. One type of material we try to cover is the idea of Machine learning and how it can affect robots and how they respond to it accordingly.
